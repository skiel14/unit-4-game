Pirate's Plunder

Argh, Matees! Welcome to Pirate's Plunder. Fill your ship with as much treasure you can carry without sinking!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game that is the maximum capacity your ship can carry without sinking.

There are four treasure chest options to plunder below. By clicking on an option you will add a specific amount to your total amount.

You will win the game by reaching but not exceeding your ship's maximum capacity. You lose the game if your total goes above the maximum capacity.

The value of each option is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each option.
